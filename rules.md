# 🏸 CITI Badminton Tournament

## Select your team to proceed if you are clear with the rules

## Welcome & Rules Guide

Welcome to the **CITI Corporate Badminton League**.\
Please review the rules before submitting your team pairings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 👥 Teams & Players

-   5 Teams participate.
-   Each team has **8 players**:
    -   5 Men
    -   3 Women
-   Only registered players are allowed to play.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🏆 Match Categories

-   **Men's Doubles (MD)**
-   **Women's Doubles (WD)**
-   **Mixed Doubles (XD)**

Each category maintains independent standings.

**Flow** 1. Qualifier → 1st vs 2nd (Winner → Final) 2. Eliminator → 3rd

### If Team Has 5 Men

-   All players must partner each other.
-   Only one pair may repeat once.
-   Same pair cannot play two knockout matches.

### If Team Has 4 Men

-   Each pair may play max 2 matches together.
-   Same pair cannot play two knockout matches.

### League Matches

-   Single set → 21 points
-   Win → 2 points
-   Loss → 0 points

Tie breaker: Highest score difference.

### Knockouts

-   Best of 3 sets
-   Each set → 15 points
